ABOUT

SHED is an artist run project space that supports and provides opportunities to underrepresented artists and artists of color. Located in a raw unfinished garage, exhibiting artists are encouraged to transform the space through installation artwork. Programming includes exhibitions, workshops, artist talks, and proposed events by artists. SHED gives agency to artists to achieve their creative goals and experiment with their ideas.
